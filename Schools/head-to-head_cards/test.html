Note: Keep in mind that for many small towns the margin of error is large because of small sample size. Use discretion when interpreting the data. In the Home Values section, some small, but very wealthy towns do not include specific home value numbers. Instead, they include categories like "1,000,000+" and "2,000,000+" etc. In 2013, Princeton Township merged with Princeton Borough into one town. We only included Princeton township data for both time periods. The Race/Ethnicity section only includes only the four major race and ethnicity categories. Not included are Native Hawaiian and Other Pacific Islander, American Indian and Alaska Native, and Two or more races.
